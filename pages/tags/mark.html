Tag <mark/>

Destaque partes de um texto:

<p>Do not forget to buy <mark>milk</mark> today.</p>

Do not forget to buy milk today.

Definição

    • A tag define texto que deve ser marcado ou destacado.

Atributos

A <mark> tag também suporta os atributos globais em HTML .

Exemplo

<p>Do not forget to buy <mark>milk</mark> today.</p>

<Do not forget to buy milk today.>
